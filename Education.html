About me

Zinan Zhao received the B.E. degree majoring in Engineerging Mechanics from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China, in 2014. He continued to pursue the M.S. degree in Solid Mechanics in NUAA and became a Ph.D. student through the Master-Doctorate program at NUAA in 2016. He is a visiting Ph.D. student at Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey through the China Scholarship Council Grant in 2018. His research interests include structural modeling and mechanical behavior analysis of FBARs, FBAR sensors and PM/PE composites. He received the Best Student Paper Awards at 2015/2016/2017 Symposium on Piezoelectricity, Acoustic Waves, and Device Applications. He is also the recipient of the 2017 Star Innovation Award of NUAA and the 2018 National Scholarship of Graduate Student.
